More About Us

Robust cleaning and Garbage Company is registered and well stocked with equipment and staff that will enable carrying out the cleaning and garbage collection services in Masajja division council efficiently and effectively. The company acquired a planned dumping site within Entebbe Municipality-Nkumba for disposal of the collected garbage.

The Company is aimed at protecting and preserving the environment through aiding tree planting projects within the division by facilitating environmental sensitive activities within a period of three months. The company is based on ensuring that garbage is properly disposed from the communities within the division. The company has trucks to extend such garbage collection services.

We look forward to working with Masajja Division Council
